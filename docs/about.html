About Chinedu

Who I Am

I’m an early-career cybersecurity / network security learner who takes a lab-first approach. I build small environments, break things safely, and validate fixes with Wireshark and Splunk. I recently completed the Certificate IV in Cyber Security at TAFE NSW (St Leonards, June 2025) and I’m continuing to study while growing my portfolio.

What I lack in experience, I make up for in discipline, curiosity, and follow-through. My approach is simple:

  1. learn the concept,
  2. build a small lab,
  3. test it until I understand it,
  4. document what works and why.

Recent focus

  • Setting up segmented networks and firewall rules in my VirtualBox lab (pfSense, Kali, Windows)
  • Practicing recon → enumeration → exploitation workflows safely, and capturing evidence
  • Running Packet Tracer labs to strengthen fundamentals (VLANs, routing basics, ACLs, WLAN)
  • Studying Cisco NetAcad content to tighten my networking theory
  • Self-paced CEH Exam preparation

How I’d add value in a junior role

  • Reliable execution: give me a runbook or a ticket and I’ll work through it carefully, ask clear questions, and close the loop.
  • Evidence-driven: I screenshot, capture packets, and annotate configs so others can follow my steps.
  • Security mindset: least privilege, change safely, verify results, and roll back cleanly.
  • Continuous learning: I schedule regular study blocks and treat feedback as fuel.

What I’m looking for

A junior Cybersecurity / Network Security / Network Engineer opportunity where I can learn from experienced engineers, contribute on real tasks, and grow into a dependable team member. If you’re open to someone who’s hungry to learn and ready to prove it, I’d love to talk.

Thanks for reading, and for the opportunity to earn it.
